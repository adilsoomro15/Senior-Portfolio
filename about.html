About Me

Adil Soomro, an esteemed EMT and burgeoning web developer, represents an exceptional amalgamation of compassion and technical proficiency within his professional endeavors. With three years of dedicated service as a volunteer EMT at the Marlboro First Aid Squad, Adil has cultivated a steadfast ability to maintain composure amidst exigent circumstances while delivering paramount care. Concurrently, his fervor for technological innovation led him to matriculate in the Web Design Pathway Program at Marlboro High School, where he has diligently refined his skills in constructing immersive digital landscapes. Scheduled for graduation in 2024, Adil epitomizes a steadfast commitment to excellence, deftly balancing his responsibilities in emergency medical services with his pursuit of proficiency in web development.

In addition to his academic and professional pursuits, Adil has already committed to Rutgers University, set to commence his journey in 2024. Beyond his academic accolades, Adil channels his creativity through the medium of photography and aerial videography using drones. Possessing an innate eye for detail and a keen appreciation for capturing poignant moments, he seamlessly synthesizes his technical acumen with his artistic sensibilities. Whether orchestrating behind the camera or navigating digital realms, Adil's dedication to precision and innovation renders him an invaluable asset in any capacity.
