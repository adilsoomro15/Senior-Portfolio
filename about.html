About Me

Adil Soomro is an esteemed EMT and budding web developer who combines compassion and technical skill in his professional life. He has volunteered as an EMT with the Marlboro First Aid Squad for three years, demonstrating a strong ability to stay calm in emergencies and provide excellent care. At the same time, his passion for technology led him to enroll in the Web Design Pathway Program at Marlboro High School, where he has been honing his skills in creating engaging digital experiences. Set to graduate in 2024, Adil exemplifies a commitment to excellence, skillfully balancing his EMT duties with his development as a web developer.

In addition to his academic and professional pursuits, Adil has committed to Rutgers University, beginning in 2024. Beyond his academic achievements, he expresses his creativity through photography and aerial videography using drones. With a natural eye for detail and a talent for capturing meaningful moments, he combines his technical skills with artistic sensibility. Whether working behind the camera or navigating digital spaces, Adil's dedication to precision and innovation makes him an invaluable asset in any field.
